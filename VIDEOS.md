## Earlier Mobile Robotics Prototypes

- **Hexapod Mobile Robot – Universidad de los Llanos / Robocontrol 2010**  
  Video demonstrations of a 12-degree-of-freedom hexapod mobile robot developed during my academic work at Universidad de los Llanos. This prototype is related to the Robocontrol 2010 / IV Workshop in Applied Robotics and Automation presentation on digital-gene-based locomotion control for hexapod robots.  
  Related work: *Gene Digital para Controle de Locomoção de Robô Móvel Hexápode de 12 Graus de Liberdade*, 2010.  
  Videos: [Demo 1](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XvjYEkmtms4&t=40s) · [Demo 2](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gp2z16p_pM0)

- **DNA Chip Emulation Robot – Mobile Robot Navigation Prototype**  
  Video demonstration related to the mobile robot navigation prototype associated with my 2008 publication on electronically emulated DNA chips, genetic algorithms, FPGA-based implementation, and bio-inspired control for mobile robot navigation.  
  Related publication: *Evolución de chip ADN emulado con algoritmo genético en FPGA para control de navegación de un robot móvil*, Orinoquia, 2008.  
  Video: [Watch on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-VjNZRwGtxo&t=14s)
